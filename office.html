Microsoft Excel

1-10 days

* Basics :

-Understand the Excel interface (ribbons, worksheets, cells).

-Learn about data entry and formatting (EX : 'number formats' , 'text alignment' , 'cell borders' ).

-Explore basic functions and formulas (EX : 'SUM' , 'AVERAGE' , 'COUNT').

-Learn how to create and edit simple charts and graphs.

* Intermediate :

-Learn about data sorting and filtering.

-Explore conditional formatting to highlight important data.

-Understand basic data analysis functions (EX : 'VLOOKUP' , 'HLOOKUP' , 'IF' , 'CONCATENATE' ).

-Learn how to create pivot tables and pivot charts.

-Understand data validation and drop-down lists.

* Advanced :

-Explore advanced functions (EX : 'INDEX' , 'MATCH' , 'OFFSET' , 'INDIRECT' ).

-Learn about advanced data analysis tools (EX : 'Power Query' , 'Power Pivot' ).

-Understand macros and VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) for automation.

-Explore advanced charting techniques (EX : 'combination charts' , 'sparklines' ).

-Learn how to collaborate on workbooks and protect data.

* Projects :

-Create a personal budget tracker.

-Develop a sales dashboard with pivot tables and charts.

-Automate repetitive tasks using VBA.

Microsoft Word

1-7 days

* Basics :

-Understand the Word interface (ribbons, document views, navigation pane).

-Learn about text formatting (EX : 'fonts' , 'sizes' , 'styles' , 'alignment' ).

-Explore paragraph formatting (EX : 'line spacing' , 'indentation' , 'bullet points' , 'numbering').

-Learn how to insert and format images, tables, and shapes.

-Understand basic document setup (EX : 'margins', 'page orientation', 'headers', 'footers').

* Intermediate :

-Learn about styles and themes for consistent formatting.

-Explore section breaks and page numbering.

-Understand how to create and format tables of contents and indexes.

-Learn how to work with templates and custom document templates.

-Explore mail merge for bulk letters and labels.

* Advanced :

-Learn about advanced formatting techniques (EX : 'multi-level lists' , 'columns' , 'text wrapping' ).

-Understand the use of references, citations, and bibliographies.

-Explore document protection and collaboration tools (EX : 'track changes', 'comments' ).

-Learn about creating fillable forms.

Understand how to automate tasks with macros.

* projects :

-Create a professional resume using Word templates.

-Develop a formatted report with a table of contents and references.

-Design a newsletter with images, columns, and hyperlinks.

Microsoft PowerPoint

1-10 days

* Basics :

-Understand the PowerPoint interface (ribbons, slides, slide sorter).

-Learn how to create and format slides (EX : 'layouts' , 'placeholders' , 'themes' ).

-Explore basic text formatting and alignment.

-Learn how to insert images, shapes, and icons.

-Understand how to create simple transitions and animations.

* Intermediate :

-Learn about slide master and template creation.

-Explore advanced animation techniques (EX : 'custom paths' , 'triggers' ).

-Understand how to work with multimedia (EX : 'inserting videos' , 'audio').

-Learn about charts and diagrams (EX : 'SmartArt' , 'organizational charts' ).

-Explore collaboration tools (EX : 'comments' , 'co-authoring' ).

* Advanced :

-Learn about advanced presentation techniques (EX : 'rehearse timings' , 'slide show setup' ).

-Explore linking and embedding content (EX : ' Excel charts' , 'Word documents').

-Understand how to create interactive presentations (EX : 'hyperlinks', 'action buttons').

-Learn about creating self-running presentations and exporting to video.

-Explore PowerPoint automation with macros.

* Projects :

-Create a business presentation with a professional template.

-Develop a product demo with interactive elements.

-Design a pitch deck with animations and multimedia integration.
